# Presentation AI Prompt: NeuraView

Copy and paste the prompt below into a Presentation AI (like Gamma.app, Tome.app, or ChatGPT with a PPT plugin) to generate a professional deck for the **NeuraView** project.

---

## 🚀 The AI Prompt

**Role:** You are a senior product manager and startup consultant preparing a pitch deck for a revolutionary AI-powered career coaching platform.

**Project Name:** NeuraView
**Tagline:** Bridging the Gap Between Preparation and Placement
**Mission:** To empower candidates with high-fidelity, real-time AI mock interviews and behavioral coaching.

**Structure the presentation into 10-12 slides with the following flow:**

1. **Title Slide:** "NeuraView: The Future of AI-Powered Career Coaching". Include "Presented by Team Limitless".
2. **The Problem:** Explain the "Preparation Gap"—how traditional mock interviews are subjective, expensive, and lack real-time behavioral feedback.
3. **The Solution:** Introduce NeuraView as an all-in-one preparation ecosystem using multimodal AI (Voice + Vision + Text).
4. **Core Feature - AI Interviews:** Highlight the Sarvam AI-powered voice interaction, ultra-low latency (<1.2s), and multilingual support (English, Hindi, etc.).
5. **Core Feature - Behavioral Analytics:** Detail the Real-time Facial & Body Language analysis using MediaPipe/Face Mesh for confidence and nervousness scoring.
6. **Core Feature - Resume & Doc Intelligence:** Mention the AI Resume Scanner for insights on resume and improvement tips and the Smart Summarizer that can summarize any document.
7. **The User Journey:** Preparation (Docs) -> Setup (Role/Difficulty) -> Live Interview -> Comprehensive Analysis & Growth.
8. **Technical Excellence (Tech Stack):** 
   - **Frontend:** Next.js 14, Framer Motion, Tailwind CSS.
   - **Backend:** Strapi 5, PostgreSQL.
   - **AI Core:** Sarvam AI (Voice/Docs), Pollinations LLM, TensorFlow.js (Vision).
9. **Impact & Growth:** Performance reports with actionable feedback and dynamic AI-generated career roadmaps.
10. **Conclusion & Vision:** NeuraView as the standard for recruitment preparation. "Mirror your excellence."

**Aesthetic Guidelines:**
- **Theme:** Modern, Futuristic, and Premium.
- **Color Palette:** Sleek Dark Mode with accents of Electric Blue, Deep Purple, and Neon Cyan (Glassmorphism style).
- **Imagery:** High-tech HUD interfaces, human-AI interaction visuals, and professional data visualization charts.
- **Tone:** Professional, innovative, and encouraging.

---

## 🛠️ How to use this prompt

1. **Gamma.app:** Choose "Create with AI" -> "Text to Presentation" -> Paste the prompt above.
2. **ChatGPT (Plus/Pro):** Paste the prompt and ask it to "Generate a detailed outline and slide-by-slide content for a PowerPoint presentation, and provide a VBA macro to create it."
3. **Canva Magic Design:** Paste the prompt into the "Magic Design" search bar for presentations.
